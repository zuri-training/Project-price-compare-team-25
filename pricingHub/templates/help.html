How do I compare things side-by-side?

Creating a side-by-side comparison on PricingHub is a great way to directly compare detailed information for various products. To create a side-by-side comparison click the compare button on any product page or product listing. Continue to select products until you have collected all of the products you want to compare. Now click compare now button to compare the products you have selected. you can also view and modify products in your compare list by clicking on compare queue in the user menu.
