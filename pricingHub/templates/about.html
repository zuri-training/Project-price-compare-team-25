About Us

We are a team of Nigeria's independent price comparison service with over 10,000 products.

Great offers every day - compare prices and offers from over 2000 retailers. Prices updated daily on all our sites. Compare prices, including shipping, from foreign countries.

We are an ambitious team who work to develop the best service for price comparison shopping. PricingHub is completely independent; we are not owned by or dependent on any manufacturer, retailer or other organisation.

At PricingHub, we offer consumers a fantastic service. You can be rest assured that we are always on your side as a consumer in the online jungle of retailers and products. We love what we do and are incredibly proud to help people make wise decisions and save a lot of money - every day of the year.

Free to Use

For you as a consumer, it's free to use our price and product comparison service. Our mission is to guide you to make the best purchase, when you have decided on one.
